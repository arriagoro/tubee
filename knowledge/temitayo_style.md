# Temitayo Agoro (@arriagoro) — Editing Style Profile

## Color Grading
- Warm cinematic tones — slight orange/amber push
- Teal-orange complementary split
- Muted shadows, lifted blacks (faded filmic look)
- Rich warm skin tones, never oversaturated
- Subtle clarity/sharpening on subject

## Text / Captions
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif, all caps (Montserrat / similar)
- **Color:** Yellow-green/lime neon with subtle glow/outer luminance
- **Style:** Word-by-word karaoke sync to speech
- **Position:** Lower-center frame
- **Size:** Compact, readable, not overpowering
- **CTA moments:** Larger hand-drawn/brush stroke text, diagonal, fills upper frame ("LINK IN THE BIO")

## Cuts & Pacing
- Hard jump cuts — NO transitions (no dissolves, no wipes)
- Speech-synced cuts — each cut aligns with a new word/phrase
- 0.5–1 second per cut on fast sections
- Beat-matched when music present
- Energy builds toward CTA climax

## Composition
- Vertical 9:16 (Instagram Reels / TikTok native)
- Subject center-framed
- Medium close-up, chest-level
- Slight reframing between cuts for visual variety (wider → tighter)
- Shallow depth of field / bokeh backgrounds

## Effects
- MINIMAL — clean, not flashy
- No heavy VFX, overlays, or motion graphics
- Possible subtle sharpening on subject
- No lower thirds, no logos in frame
- Equipment as prop (gimbal = credibility)

## Overall Aesthetic
- "Aspirational Creator-Lifestyle" — cinematic quality with casual energy
- High production value but feels authentic/personal
- Luxury settings reinforce brand
- Personality-first — the person IS the content
- Clean minimalist editing, NOT over-edited

## What Tubee Should Do For This Style
1. Hard cuts only — no transitions
2. Warm cinematic color grade (teal-orange, lifted blacks)
3. Caption overlay: bold sans-serif, lime/yellow-green, word-by-word sync
4. Keep cuts tight: 0.5–2 seconds
5. Build energy toward a climax/CTA moment
6. Center-frame compositions
7. NO cheesy effects — keep it clean and professional
